Mission

Our mission at Marlow's Contrabands is to democratize access to education and technology. We believe that everyone, regardless of their background or financial status, should have the opportunity to learn and grow. By providing free and open-source software solutions, we aim to empower individuals to unlock their full potential and make a positive impact on the world.

Education to make the world better should be free

At Marlow's Contrabands, we believe that education is a fundamental human right. That's why we're committed to providing free access to high-quality educational resources for everyone. Our mission is to empower individuals from all walks of life to learn, grow, and make a positive impact on the world. Join us in our quest to make education accessible to all!

Our standings

  1. Professionals and freelancers should have access to free tools.
  2. Students shouldn't have to pay for software because they are still in the learning process.
  3. Open-source software fosters innovation and collaboration within the community.
  4. Access to free tools promotes inclusivity and equal opportunities for all individuals, regardless of their financial background.

openCAD

openCAD is a free and open-source Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software aimed at providing accessible tools for engineers, architects, and designers. With a user-friendly interface and powerful features, openCAD allows users to create detailed 2D and 3D models for various engineering and architectural projects. Whether you're designing mechanical parts, architectural structures, or electronic circuits, openCAD offers the flexibility and versatility you need to bring your ideas to life.

openCFD

openCFD is a computational fluid dynamics (CFD) software designed to simulate and analyze fluid flow phenomena. Our modeler provides advanced scalability and personalization options, allowing engineers and researchers to tackle complex fluid dynamics problems with ease. From aerodynamics simulations to heat transfer analysis, openCFD empowers users to explore a wide range of applications in fluid mechanics. Join our community today and dive into the world of computational fluid dynamics with openCFD.

HUBArch

HUBArch is an innovative architecture software that leverages the power of artificial intelligence (AI) to assist architects in designing and optimizing building structures. Our platform offers multiple models of AI, including machine learning and deep learning algorithms, to provide architects with intelligent design recommendations and performance evaluations. With HUBArch, architects can streamline their design process, enhance creativity, and deliver sustainable and efficient architectural solutions. Join us in revolutionizing the architecture industry with HUBArch.

PIFTA

PIFTA (Policy Analysis Frameworks modeling architecture) is a comprehensive framework for modeling and analyzing policy scenarios. Our platform integrates advanced modeling techniques and data analysis tools to help policymakers and analysts evaluate the potential impacts of policy decisions. From economic policy simulations to environmental policy assessments, PIFTA provides a flexible and customizable framework for policy analysis. Join the PIFTA community and make informed policy decisions for a better future.
